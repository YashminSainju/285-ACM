Who we are

The Association for Computing Machinery, located at Southeastern Louisiana University, is a club devoted to computers, friends, and fun. With guest speakers coming to every other meeting, full of knowledge to share with the students, this organization strives to indulge both the intellectual and the socialite. Come to our next meeting to truly experience the wonder that is the SLU chapter of ACM!
